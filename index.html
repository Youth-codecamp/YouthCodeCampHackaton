Requirement!

1

Prepared project

Participants must come with a fully developed project related to web application or software development.

2

Document Upload during Registration

Each participant should upload a document showcasing details of their project during the registration process. This document should include project goals, technologies used, and any other relevant information

3

Bring Your Own equipment

Participants are required to bring their own laptops and any other necessary equipment for the project showcase.

4

Respectful Collaboration

Encourage a collaborative and respectful environment during the project showcase.

5

Project Showcase Guidelines

Follow the guidelines provided for the project showcase, including time limits for presentations and addressing questions from judges.

6

Code of Conduct

Abide by the event's code of conduct to ensure a positive and inclusive atmosphere for all participants.

7

Submission Deadline

Projects must be ready for the showcase within the specified timeframe.

8

Original Work

All projects must be original and developed prior to the hackathon
