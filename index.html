FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

What is Youth Code Camp Hackathon?

The Youth Code Camp Hackathon is an exciting event where young programmers come together to learn, collaborate, and develop their tech skills through workshops.

When and where is the Hackathon taking place?

The hackathon is scheduled for August 24th, 2024, and will be hosted at Excella School. Please check the event details for specific times and location.

Who can participate in the hackathon?

The hackathon is open to both experienced coders and beginners who are currently in high school (secondary school). Whether you're a pro or just starting out, everyone is welcome to join and contribute their skills and ideas.

How do I register for the hackathon?

To register, simply visit our website and fill out the online registration form.

Are there any registration fees?

No, participation in the Youth Code Camp Hackathon is free of charge.

What should participants prepare for registration?

Participants should be ready to provide details such as their personal information, contact details, and any other relevant information requested during the registration process.

Is there a registration deadline?

Yes, participants must register by the deadline specified for the hackathon. Late registrations may not be accepted.

What are the benefits of participating?

Participants will have the opportunity to earn verified certificates and other prizes at the event. They can also connect with fellow programmers and learn from workshops

How can I become a sponsor or partner for the hackathon?

If you are interested in becoming a sponsor or partner, please contact us at youthcodecamp@gmail.com. We welcome collaboration with organizations that share our vision for supporting young programmers.

What safety measures are in place for the event?

We prioritize the safety of our participants. We will adhere to all relevant health and safety guidelines, and any specific measures will be communicated closer to the event date.
