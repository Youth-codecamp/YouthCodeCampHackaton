FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

What is Youth Code Camp Hackathon?

The Youth Code Camp Hackathon is an exciting event that brings together young programmers to showcase their pre-developed projects, collaborate with peers, and learn from experienced mentors.

When and where is the Hackathon taking place?

The hackathon is scheduled for July 15th, 2024, and will be hosted at Lycee De Kigali. Please check the event details for specific times and location.

Who can participate in the hackathon?

The hackathon is open to both experienced coders and beginners. Whether you're a pro or just starting out, everyone is welcome to join and contribute their skills and ideas.

How do I register for the hackathon?

To register, visit our website and fill out the online registration form. During registration, you will be required to upload a document showcasing details of your project.

Can I participate if I don't have a pre-developed project?

This hackathon is specifically designed for participants to showcase projects they have developed before the event. However, we encourage you to participate in future events and engage in the collaborative coding sessions we organize.

Are there any registration fees?

No, participation in the Youth Code Camp Hackathon is free of charge.

What should be included in the project document during registration?

The project document should include details such as project goals, technologies used, and any other relevant information that gives an overview of your pre-developed project.

Is there a deadline for project submissions?

Yes, projects must be submitted within the specified timeframe provided during the hackathon. Late submissions may not be considered for evaluation.

How will projects be evaluated?

Projects will be evaluated based on criteria such as creativity, functionality, innovation, and presentation during the showcase. Judges will also consider the effectiveness of collaboration if applicable.

What are the benefits of participating?

Participants have the opportunity to showcase their projects, network with other programmers, learn from workshops, and potentially receive recognition and awards. Additionally, the hackathon provides a platform to connect with mentors and sponsors.

How can I become a sponsor or partner for the hackathon?

If you are interested in becoming a sponsor or partner, please contact us at shyakachaste1@gmail.com. We welcome collaboration with organizations that share our vision for supporting young programmers.

What safety measures are in place for the event?

We prioritize the safety of our participants. We will adhere to all relevant health and safety guidelines, and any specific measures will be communicated closer to the event date.
